Why I am interested in Fairfield

Fairfield is very determined in expanding their institution and work ethic globally. It 
        also promotes a sense of belonging, where everyone values each other

        I found the Bachelor of Science program in Computer Science appealing. This program 
        stresses that its students dive deep into their creativity and communicate
        with each other. I am a creative person who is able to communicate with anyone I work with.
        I know my creativity limits, but I know that this program will show me even more ways to
        come up with my own ideas.
